MentorPack

MentorPack is a global mentorship platform that provides young aspiring individuals with experienced mentors in the professional world. We believe it is essential for aspiring individuals to surround themselves with people that are already where they want to be.

We work with young and talented individuals to build the foundations of their professional careers by leveraging the mentor's knowledge and experience in specific fields to offer personalized guidance in order to navigate the unknown paths of the mentee's career path and help them reach their goals faster.

The Pack

The pack binds the wolves together as one. It represents devotion, respect, and extreme loyalty. The pack is a family where little wolves are taken care of, fed and nurtured. Indeed, wolves symbolize guardianship. These animals very family oriented, communicate with each other, have strong emotional attachments, and trust their own instincts. Inspired by this animal we aim to create a unique community of individuals where every member is guided and taken care to unleash their full potential.

What is mentoring?

Mentoring is a professional partnership between two parties: the mentor and the mentee.

The idea behind mentoring is knowledge sharing, career development and performance improvement.

Our mentoring program offers counseling sessions, in which the mentor and the mentee set a goal to achieve, through a specific framework and defined timeline.

What does MentorPack offer?

  • One-on-one mentoring
  • Group mentoring
  • Workshops
  • Personalized plans
  • Distance or E-mentoring
